Netcrawler is a series of libraries in various languages (only a couple for now, but more to come eventually, the idea
is to support every language I know), which provides convenient functionality for web crawling and data grabbing
from various services, such as Wikipedia page objects which handles searching and gives quick access to information, search
engine crawlers to handle parsing of results from search engines such as DuckDuckGo, Startpage, and Qwant, in a unified API,
as well as various other ideas, all in a single place with a simple to use syntax, and automatically handling caching
to prevent spamming of online services.
